This is one of the assigments we ask candidates to do on a job interview. It's a test that allows you to show your bootstrap skills. This is an angular project using bootsrap 4. If you take a peek at the assignments page, you will see a grid which has some fixed columns. Your task is to make the grid responsive.

The first row shows two columns of the same size. The first column is more important and should become relatively more important when the viewport decreases in size. On a small view, the second column must disappear.

The second row shows two columns of a different size. When the viewport becomes smaller (medium), both columns should become the same size. In the smallest view, the entire row must disappear.

The thirt row shows three columns of equal size. Once the view becomes smaller (medium or less) the columns must stretch out to the entire page width, and stack.
